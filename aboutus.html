About Foot'n'Cups

FOOT 'N' CUPS

Foot'N'Cups is a 5 a-side Astroturf football pitch in Ikorodu Selewu in Lagos. Foot'N'Cups promotes 5-aside soccer among the teaming population of football fans in Nigeria. We operate FIFA-standard pitches and deploy certified officials in safe and secure locations across Lagos.

our services

FOOT'N'CUPS ADVANTAGE

Our Mission as a sports company, is to promote 5-aside soccer among the teaming population of football fans in Nigeria and across the globe. At FOOT'N'CUPS, we provide a safe and exciting arena for football enthusiast to display thier talent and we also create an avenue for young talents an opportunity of being professional footballers. We run a second to none state of the art facility.
